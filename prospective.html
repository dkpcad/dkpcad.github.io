Prospective Students

Prospective PhD students

The PhD is an interesting journey which requires faith in your mentor and yourself too. The end goals of the PhD are (1) Transforming your own self-image and (2) Doing independent high-quality research with international collaborators. There are ample things to be discussed before initiating a PhD. If interested, please reach out to me via an email and we can proceed the discussion with Skype or in person meeting.

A message to prospective BTech / MTech Students

I would greatly appreciate UG or PG student(s) to approach me if interested in exploring following research areas. I would be happy to host you in MICxN Research Lab and talk further about opportunities. We can set up an online or in person meeting. Student can explore UGRP project details for further clarity and drop an email to kick start the conversations.
