UGRP-5 Written Test Result (February 8) and Further Instructions:

  1. The shortlisting criteria for Part 1 and Part 2 are based on ANY ONE Criteria A or B:

    1. Criteria A: Minimum 50 percentage total score of all FOUR sections: 1. Aptitude 2. English 3. Programming 4. Mathematics

    2. Criteria B: Minimum 50 percentile according to the weighted average of the scores of test sections and CGPA as:

      1. 10% English

      2. 10% Aptitude

      3. 30% Programming

      4. 30% Mathematics

      5. 20% CGPA

  2. The following students are shortlisted for the Personal Interactions planned on February 12 (Sunday). No email notifications will be sent. UGRP aspirants are requested to follow the sequence defined in Parts 1 and 2.

  3. For Part 1, students are required to remain present at 10 AM, Faculty office 210.

  4. For Part 2, students are required to remain present at 10 AM, MICxN Research Lab 300. There will be two panels for the discussions.

  5. Each student may require 15-20-25 minutes (max). Please feel free to go ahead and plan your schedule accordingly.

  6. For Part 3, students are not required to remain present. However, a separate personal group meeting will be conducted next week, likely Feb 14 (Wednesday) at 3.30 PM, COM Lab 205. Please be sure to remain present for the further set of instructions.

  7. Based on the personal interactions and follow up meeting, the final list will be updated soon. For Part 1 and Part 3, it has been updated. Part 2 students will get the seprate notifications in campuswire group. For Part 3, a sperate campuswire group has been created.
