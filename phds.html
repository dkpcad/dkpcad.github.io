Brijesh Soni [2017]

Brijesh Soni is currently working as Junior Research Fellow at School of Engineering and Applied Science, Ahmedabad University for Department of Science and Technology (DST) - UK-India Education and Research Initiative (UKIERI) research project jointly funded by DST and British Council. He received the B.E. degree in Electronics Engineering from B.V.M Engineering college and M.E. degrees in Communication Systems Engineering from L.D College of Engineering (both with Distinction) in 2014 and 2017, respectively. He is also currently pursuing his Ph.D. degree in Information, Communication and Technology at School of Engineering and Applied Science, Ahmedabad University. His research interests include Cognitive Radio Networks, Machine learning/Deep learning for xG wireless networks, Speech Enhancement and Physical Layer Security.

Email: brijesh.soni@ahduni.edu.in
