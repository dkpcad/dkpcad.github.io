About Me

Dr. Dhaval Patel is currently working as Assistant Professor at School of Engineering and Applied Science-Ahmedabad University, India. He was also a visiting faculty at Olin School of Engineering, Massachusetts, United states. He has published research papers in IEEE Journals and Tier-1 International Conferences. He is contributing his services as a review editor for Frontiers in Space Technologies. He is a member of Editorial Board of Aerial and Space Networks. He has been awarded with Ahmedabad University Chairman's Award for Research for outstanding contributions. Furthermore, he has received research funding from DST-UK India and British Council, DST- Association of Southeast Asian Nations and Gujarat Council on Science and Technology. Furthermore, he is a member of professional bodies like IEEE, ISTE and IETE.

His area of interest includes Intelligent Transportation System, Bio-Engineering, Molecular Communication, Applied Machine Learning and Deep Learning, and xG Wireless Networks.
